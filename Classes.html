Classes

Choose Your Class!

Barbarian

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Want to be on the front lines
  • Consider combat one of their priorities
  • Want a beefy and brawny character

View more details below!

Bard

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Prioritize roleplay
  • Enjoy a lot of freedom in what direction they take their character
  • Enjoy having many skills and being good at them
  • Like “gimmicky” or silly spells

View more details below!

Cleric

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Like playing support and healing the party
  • Like themed characters (see: domains)
  • Like always having something they can do (heal, attack, buff, etc)
  • Want to build a character who is devout (even in an unorthodox way)
  • Are having a hard time choosing their character and none of the decided players in the party are classes that can heal or want to heal
  • Are in a campaign that involves fighting vampires or the undead

View more details below!

Druid

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Enjoy nature orientated characters
  • Like animals! Plants! Theming!
  • Enjoy shapeshifting characters

View more details below!

Fighter

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Prioritize combat over all else
  • Want to multiclass at some point (an advanced topic we do not address)
  • Are new to the game and scared of complicating things

View more details below!

Monk

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Want to be skilled at combat, but also value a roleplay direction to take their character
  • Want to make a martial arts based character
  • Like having ridiculous Naruto tier physical abilities for your character
  • Enjoyed Avatar the Last Airbender

View more details below!

Paladin

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Enjoy the versatility of a front-line fighter who can cast spells
  • Are interested in making a devout character (even if in an unorthodox way)
  • Will be facing fiends or undead during their campaign

View more details below!

Ranger

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Are looking to be the person to go to for stealthing
  • Also are into the nature aesthetic, even if it’s for killing things

View more details below!

Rogue

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Enjoy dealing a lot of damage
  • Like the aspect of hiding or stealthing
  • Are making a character who enjoys stealing, treasure hunting, or both

View more details below!

Sorcerer

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Want to make a character who gained their magical powers through an incident or from birthright
  • Like to cast a lot of spells
  • Want a class that emphasizes spellcasting that doesn’t have to wait for the slow gain of knowledge

View more details below!

Sorcerer

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Want to make a character who received their powers from a patron
  • Possibly want to be a frontline fighter whose powers come from their spells and charisma
  • Like getting the unique bonuses to both roleplay and combat that vary per patron
  • Likes having the opportunity to cast more spells at the cost of learning more spells

View more details below!

Wizard

This class is recommended for players who:

  • Want to make a character who developed their magical powers through education
  • Are patient - Wizards are a slow climb but become one of the most powerful damage dealers in the game

View more details below!
