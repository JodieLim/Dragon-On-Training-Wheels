Armor

To protect yourself from weapons, you need armor. Armor can be understood in terms of armor class, and armor type. There are also some armor attributes to keep in mind. Once you understand armor, Lily will show you how to equip them!

Armor Class: How To Protect Your Boy Body

In DND, you resist physical attacks using your ARMOR CLASS (AC) .

The value of your armor class depends on the armor you're wearing and a few other factors.

Think of AC as how easy or hard it is to land a hit on you. Having thick armor can help you with that, but so can a nimble body or arcane force.


When someone attacks in DND...

If a number is less than the target’s AC, then it does not hit.
If a number is greater than the target’s AC, then it hits.
If a number is equal to the target’s AC, the damage is halved.


Sometimes AC is not determined just by the armor you wear.

1) Unarmored Defense

Barbarian's AC = 10 + DEX Modifier + CON Modifier

Monk's AC = 10 + DEX Modifier + WIS Modifier

2) Spells

Sorcerers and Wizards can learn Mage Armor, which makes the target's AC = 13 + DEX for the spell's duration.

Paladins and Clerics can learn Shield of Faith which adds +2 to the target's AC for the spell's duration.
