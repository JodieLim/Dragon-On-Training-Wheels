Armor

To protect yourself from weapons, you need armor. Armor can be understood in terms of armor class, and armor type. There are also some armor attributes to keep in mind. Once you understand armor, Lily will show you how to equip them!

Armor Class: How To Protect Your Boy Body

In DND, you resist physical attacks using your ARMOR CLASS (AC) .
The value of your armor class depends on the armor you're wearing and a few other factors.

Think of AC as how easy or hard it is to land a hit on you. Having thick armor can help you with that, but so can a nimble body or arcane force.


When someone attacks in DND...

If a number is less than the target’s AC, then it does not hit.

If a number is greater than the target’s AC, then it hits.
If a number is equal to the target’s AC, then it hits.


Some Dungeon Masters may play with different home rules. Be sure to ask your DM!


Armor Proficiency

You should wear armor that your class is proficient in.
There are a lot of consequences if you don't!

If you wear armor you are not proficient in...
  • You have disadvantage (you have to roll twice and take the lower number) on any roll that involves Strength or Dexterity
  • You cannot cast spells.

Sometimes AC is not determined just by the armor you wear.

1) Unarmored Defense

Barbarian's AC = 10 + DEX Modifier + CON Modifier

Monk's AC = 10 + DEX Modifier + WIS Modifier

2) Spells

Sorcerers and Wizards can learn Mage Armor, which makes the target's AC = 13 + DEX for the spell's duration.

Paladins and Clerics can learn Shield of Faith which adds +2 to the target's AC for the spell's duration.

Armor Attributes: Strong 'n' Stealthy

Some heavy armor may have strength requirements. Some armor puts you at a DISADVANTAGE (you must roll twice and take the lower number) on stealth checks.

Strength Requirement: Are You A Strong Boy?

Some heavy armor may have strength requirements.
Chain Mail requires STR 13
Splint Mail requires STR 15
Plate Mail requires STR 15


Stealth Disadvantage: Are You A Noisy Boy?

Some armor puts you at a DISADVANTAGE (you must roll twice and take the lower number) on stealth checks.

The following armor inflicts stealth disadvantage:

LIGHT
  • Padded
MEDIUM
  • Scale
  • Half
HEAVY
  • Ring Mail
  • Chain Mail
  • Splint Mail
  • Plate Mail
