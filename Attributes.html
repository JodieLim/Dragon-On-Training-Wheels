Dexterity

Hank is the CLUMSIEST MAN ALIVE!

Hank probably actually has two left feet!

Hank is not too clumsy, not too agile!

Hank is pretty good on his feet!

Hank is SPIDERHANK!

A measure of agility, reflexes, and balance. One of the most important attributes in the game, as it determines many different things.

Dexterity is useful for

  • Ranged weapons users such as
    • Fighters
    • Rangers
  • Spellcasters who have ranged weapons as their alternate way of doing damage
  • Finesse weapons user such as
    • Bards
    • Monks
      • When a monk uses a monk weapon, they use dexterity.
    • Rogues
  • Armor Class
    • Depending on the armor you wear, your dexterity modifier may or may not come into play.
    • Monks have all of their armor decided by their dexterity and wisdom.
  • Having high initiative
    • Everytime you go into battle, you roll initiative, which uses your dex modifier.
    • The higher the initiative, the earlier your turn to attack is.
  • The following checks
    • Enduring certain magical spells
    • Having quick reflexes or a good reaction time
    • Disabling a trap
  • The following skills
    • Acrobatics
    • Stealth
    • Sleight of Hand

A measure of perceptiveness, intuition, common sense, and your general knowledge of the world around you.

Wisdom is useful for

  • Spellcasters who use wisdom to cast spells
    • Clerics

    • Rangers
